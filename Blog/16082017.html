Studying efficiently with tech configurations

August 16, 2017

I've been carrying my Macbook Pro 15 and iPad Pro to school everyday. I use notability for note taking and working log. Problems here are: my backpack would be heavy and Macbook could be really hot when I continuously do heavy stuff.

To solve this, I bought a desktop placed in my office at school running Windows. I use Dropbox for daily backup of my recent files. Now I can just commute with my iPad Pro. Life is saved.

The only issue is Windows doesn't have notability which now I only use for note taking with an apple pencil. I just use Notepad to write my worklog and save it to Dropbox and organize to notability later.
